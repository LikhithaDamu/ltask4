About Us

We are passionate about crafting and offer a wide variety of products for all your creative needs. From paint sets to brushes and more, we have everything you need to create beautiful handmade crafts!

Craft Store of India started in 2019 with the idea of building an ecosystem where our crafting community can get quality and unique craft supplies in the most affordable and hassle-free way. At the same time give inspiration or get inspired to be more creative.

We believe in innovation and learning from our community. We currently sell more than 2,000+ products via our website and our 12,000+ customer base constantly help us to do better every day.

We are proud that we are:

100% organically grown business

Support more than 200 artisans/workers directly and indirectly

Ship to every pincode in India so that no crafter feels left-out

4.9/5 star with more than 800+ reviews on our Google Page

60,000+ crafting community on our Instagram

Known for our packaging and fast delivery
